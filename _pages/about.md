# Welcome

I am Enhao Cui, a Ph.D. candidate at the College of Chemistry, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.

My research focuses on quantum-dot-based optoelectronic synaptic devices, neuromorphic vision systems, and photoelectric materials for in-sensor computing.

My current work involves the construction and performance regulation of quantum dot visual devices for sensing-memory computing, with particular interest in perovskites, quantum dots, optoelectronic synapses, interfacial charge trapping, and near-infrared neuromorphic vision.

## Research Interests

* Optoelectronic synaptic devices
* Quantum-dot-based neuromorphic vision
* Perovskite and quantum dot photoelectric materials
* Interface charge trapping and carrier transport
* In-sensor computing and visual perception
* Carbon quantum dots and trace metal ion detection
* Photocatalytic materials for environmental applications
